Patient information

At the core of everything we do is our belief that quality essential healthcare should be accessible to everybody. We deliver affordable, world-class care that enhances the health of every person we come into contact with. Whether delivered by us or through contracted, trusted accredited providers, we provide a seamless journey of high quality care.

Online appointment booking

Book appointments online at your convenience - day or night – with medical practitioners and dentists at Medicross centres.

Practice opportunities

Offering practice management and support for healthcare practitioners


To help private healthcare professionals focus what matters most, we provide a comprehensive suite of practice and administrative management services. We take the load of to enable healthcare providers better serve their patients.

A commitment to Quality of Care

It’s not just something we say. We regularly monitor, measure and publish the data that shows the high quality of care we provide, guided always by our principles of transparent and patient-centred care.

Get in touch

As part of our commitment to providing the best and safest care to all of our patients, we listen. If you have anything you’d like to ask us, any problems or complaints, any compliments or other feedback, please get in touch.
